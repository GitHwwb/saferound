Why SafeRound?

Built for finance professionals, analysts, and anyone who needs clean numbers without compromising accuracy.

Precise Rounding

Rounds values to any decimal precision while maintaining the exact total sum. No more penny discrepancies.

Flexible Output

Overwrite existing data or output results to a new sheet. Preserve totals across columns or rows — your choice.

Simple Interface

A clean sidebar UI with range selection. Highlight cells, pick your precision, and go. Also available as a custom function.

fx =SAFEROUND(A1:A10, 0)

Also available as a custom function — use it directly in your formulas.
