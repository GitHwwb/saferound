Privacy & Terms

How SafeRound handles your data and the terms governing its use.

Privacy Policy

What Permissions Does SafeRound Request?

SafeRound requests the following permissions:

  • View and edit your spreadsheets in Google Sheets™ (/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly) — Required so the add-on can read selected ranges, apply rounding logic, and write adjusted values back to your spreadsheet.
  • Access to specific files you use with this app in Google Drive™ (/auth/drive.file) — Allows SafeRound to operate only on spreadsheets you explicitly open and use with the add-on. It does not provide access to other files in your Google Drive™.
  • Display custom interfaces inside Google Sheets™ (/auth/script.container.ui) — Enables SafeRound to open a sidebar interface where you can select ranges, choose rounding options, and run the tool. No personal data is collected through this interface.

What Data Does SafeRound Access or Store?

SafeRound only processes the data you select in your sheet while using the add-on. It does not:

  • Collect, store, or share any personal or spreadsheet data externally
  • Use external servers or third-party APIs
  • Track user behavior or collect analytics

All processing happens within the Google Sheets™ environment and is performed locally using Google Apps Script.

How Is My Data Used?

Data from your spreadsheet is:

  • Temporarily read for rounding and total preservation logic
  • Written back into your sheet based on the output method you choose (e.g., overwrite or new sheet)

Once the operation is complete, no data is retained.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app's functionality or permissions change. Significant updates will be noted within the add-on or on our landing page.
